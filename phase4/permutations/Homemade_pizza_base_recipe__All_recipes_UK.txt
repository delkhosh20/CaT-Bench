========
1. Add ingredients in the order suggested by your manufacturer.
2. Set for dough setting and start machine.
3. When the unit signals , remove dough.
4. Pat dough into a greased 30cm ( 12 in ) round pizza pan.
5. Let stand 10 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 200 C / Gas mark 6.
7. Spread pizza sauce over dough.
8. Sprinkle toppings over sauce ( such as basil and mozzarella for a simple margherita pizza ).
9. Bake 15 to 20 minutes , or until crust is golden brown.
