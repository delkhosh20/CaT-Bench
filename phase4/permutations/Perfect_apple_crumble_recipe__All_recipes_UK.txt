1. Put the apple chunks into a pan , add three quarters of the sugar , the cinnamon stick or clove and cover.
2. Allow to stew gently for half an hour , stirring occasionally with a wooden spoon.
3. Keep checking as they can easily burn at the beginning.
4. Check that the fruit is cooked by mashing it a bit with a wooden spoon.
5. The apples should be soft but not purÃ©ed.
6. Put the apples into the serving dish and allow to cool.
7. Set the temperature of the oven to 150 C / gas 2.
8. Mix the flour , remaining sugar and the butter with your fingers in a bowl until it becomes like a breadcrumb consistency.
9. Cover the stewed apples with the crumble topping , without pressing down.
10. Bake for approximately 30 minutes or until the crumble is golden brown and crunchy.
11. Spoon a helping of crumble into a dish and serve with cream or ice cream.
