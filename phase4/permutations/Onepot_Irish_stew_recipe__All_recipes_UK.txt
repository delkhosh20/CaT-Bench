========
1. Place the lamb in a large pot , cover with water and add a pinch of salt.
2. Bring to the boil ; skim off any white fat that comes to the surface , then simmer on low heat for another 30 minutes.
3. Add the onion and half of the potatoes and cook for 30 minutes until the potatoes are soft ; break them up and stir - this will thicken the stew.
4. Add the remaining potatoes and carrots.
5. Simmer for another 20-30 minutes until potatoes are cooked.
6. Season well with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Stir in fresh parsley or chopped leeks.
8. Serve piping hot.
