========
1. Cook the pasta in a large saucepan of lightly salted boiling water for 10-12 minutes.
2. Drain well in a colander.
3. Place the pasta in a large mixing bowl and toss well with the olive oil.
4. Drain the tuna and use a fork to roughly break it into flakes.
5. Put the tuna into a blender or food processor.
6. Add the lemon juice to taste , capers or wine vinegar , and mayonnaise and blend together until smooth and creamy.
7. Add to the pasta and stir together until all the ingredients are evenly mixed.
8. De-seed the pepper and then finely dice.
9. Finely dice the cucumber without removing the skin.
10. Add the pepper , cucumber and sweetcorn to the pasta and toss together.
11. Spoon into a serving dish.
12. Remove the shell from the hard-boiled eggs and cut into quarters.
13. Arrange the wedges decoratively over the salad , then serve.
