========
1. In a casserole , cook sausage , mince , onion and garlic over medium heat until well browned.
2. Stir in chopped tomatoes , tomato puree , passata and water.
3. Season with sugar , basil , fennel seeds , Italian herbs , 1 tablespoon salt , pepper and 2 tablespoons parsley.
4. Simmer , covered , for about 1 1/2 hours , stirring occasionally.
5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to the boil.
6. Cook lasagne sheets in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes.
7. Drain sheets , and rinse with cold water.
8. In a mixing bowl , combine ricotta cheese with egg , remaining parsley and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
9. Preheat oven to 190 C / Gas mark 5.
10. To assemble , spread 375ml of meat sauce in the bottom of a 22x33cm ( 9x13 in ) baking dish.
11. Arrange 6 lasagne sheets lengthways over meat sauce.
12. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture.
13. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
14. Spoon another 375ml of meat sauce over mozzarella , and sprinkle with 1/3 of the Parmesan cheese.
15. Repeat layers , and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
16. Cover with aluminium foil ( to prevent sticking , either grease the foil with oil , or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese ).
17. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
18. Remove foil , and bake an additional 25 minutes.
19. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
