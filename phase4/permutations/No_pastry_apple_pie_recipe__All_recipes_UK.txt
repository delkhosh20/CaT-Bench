========
1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4.
2. Lightly grease a 23cm pie dish with margarine.
3. Fill 2/3 of the dish with sliced apples.
4. Sprinkle with cinnamon and 1 teaspoon sugar.
5. In a medium bowl , mix 200g sugar with the melted margarine.
6. Stir in pecans , flour , egg and salt.
7. Mix well.
8. Spread mixture over the apples.
9. Bake in preheated oven for 65 minutes , or until golden brown.
