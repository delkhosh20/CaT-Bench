1. In a mixing bowl , combine peanut butter , soy sauce , lime juice , sugar , curry powder , garlic and hot sauce.
2. Place the chicken in the marinade and refrigerate.
3. Let the chicken marinate at least 2 hours ; overnight is best.
4. Preheat barbecue to high heat.
5. Thread the chicken onto bamboo skewers , then cook for 5 minutes per side.
