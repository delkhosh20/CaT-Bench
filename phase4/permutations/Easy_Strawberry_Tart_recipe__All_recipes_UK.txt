========
1. Mix together the sugar , water , jam and cornflour in a medium large saucepan.
2. Boil for one minute.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Stir in the sliced strawberries , and toss until well coated.
5. Pour into the digestive biscuit base.
6. Chill until well set.
7. Serve topped with whipped cream.
