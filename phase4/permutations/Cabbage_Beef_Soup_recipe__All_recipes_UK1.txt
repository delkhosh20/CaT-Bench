======== Original Recipe ========
1. Heat oil in a large stockpot over medium high heat.
2. Add mince and onion , and cook until beef is well browned and crumbled.
3. Drain fat , and transfer beef to a slow cooker.
4. Add cabbage , kidney beans , water , passata , stock cubes , cumin , salt and pepper.
5. Stir to dissolve stock cubes , and cover.
6. Cook on high setting for 4 hours , or on low setting for 6 to 8 hours.
7. Stir occasionally.
8. Enjoy !
