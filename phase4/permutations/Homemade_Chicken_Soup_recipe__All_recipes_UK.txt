========
1. Place the chicken , carrots , celery and onion in a large soup pot ; cover with cold water.
2. Heat and simmer , uncovered , until the chicken meat falls off of the bones ( skim off foam every so often ).
3. Take everything out of the pot ; sieve the stock.
4. Pick the meat off of the bones and chop the carrots , celery and onion.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Return the chicken , carrots , celery and onion to the pot , stir together , and serve.
