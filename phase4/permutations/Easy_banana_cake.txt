========
1. 1. Grease and line a 2lb loaf tin.
2. 2. Melt butter , sugar and vanilla in a saucepan over a medium heat.
3. 3. Remove from heat and add the mashed bananas , mix well.
4. 4. Add the egg , mix well.
5. 5. Stir in the flour and the milk.
6. 6. Pour into the prepared tin , sprinkle with a tablespoon of demerara sugar to give a crunch topping if liked.
7. 7. Bake at 170 C / Fan 150 C / Gas 3 for 35 minutes , or until a skewer comes out clean.
8. 8. Leave to cool and enjoy !
