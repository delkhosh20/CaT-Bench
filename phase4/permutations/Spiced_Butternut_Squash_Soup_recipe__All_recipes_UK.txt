========
1. Preheat the oven to 190 C / Gas mark 6.
2. Pour a small layer of water in a baking dish , or a swiss roll tin.
3. Place the squash halves cut side down on the dish.
4. Bake for about 40 minutes , or until a fork can easily pierce the flesh.
5. Cool slightly , then remove the peel.
6. Set aside.
7. Melt the butter in a large pot over medium heat.
8. Add the onion , leek and garlic , and saute for a few minutes , until tender.
9. Pour the chicken stock into the pot.
10. Add the potatoes , and bring to the boil.
11. Cook for about 20 minutes , or until soft.
12. Add the squash , and mash with the potatoes until chunks are small.
13. Use an immersion hand blender to puree the soup , or transfer to a blender or food processor in batches , and puree until smooth.
14. Return to the pot.
15. Season the soup with cayenne pepper , allspice , nutmeg , ginger , salt and pepper , then stir in the sherry , cream and milk.
16. Heat through , but do not boil.
17. Ladle into bowls , and top with a dollop of soured cream if desired.
