========
1. Preheat the oven to 180 C / Gas 4.
2. Line a large loaf tin with a liner.
3. Alternatively you can grease with unsalted butter and line with baking paper , but a liner will produce far better results.
4. Sift the flour , baking powder , sugar and cornflour into a large mixing bowl and beat with an electric hand whisk until evenly mixed.
5. Add the butter , eggs , lemon zest and the 3 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice.
6. Whisk again until fully combined and smooth.
7. Pour the mixture into the pre-lined load tin and level the top with a spatula or the back of a spoon.
8. Make the drizzle by stirring the caster sugar and the 5 tablespoons lemon juice together in a jug.
9. Prick the surface of the cooled loaf all over with a fork.
10. Pour the drizzle over the loaf and allow for it to set ( once set , the drizzle will be hard ) before removing the loaf from the tin and serving.
11. Enjoy ! : )
