========
1. 1. Cut the meat , across the grain , into 5cm wide pieces , and then cut each piece into wafer-thin strips.
2. 2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. 3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. 4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. 5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. 6. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
7. 7. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
8. 8. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
9. 9. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
10. 10. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
11. 11. Remove with a draining spoon.
12. 12. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
13. 13. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
14. 14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. 15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.
