======== Original Recipe ========
1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas mark 4.
2. Cream together the butter , caster sugar and brown sugar until smooth.
3. Beat in the eggs one at a time , then stir in the vanilla.
4. Dissolve bicarbonate of soda in hot water.
5. Add to batter along with salt.
6. Stir in flour , chocolate chips and nuts.
7. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased baking trays.
8. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven , or until edges are nicely browned.
