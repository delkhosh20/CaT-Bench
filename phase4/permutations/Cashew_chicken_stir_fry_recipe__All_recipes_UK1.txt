======== Original Recipe ========
1. Dissolve the cornflour in the chicken stock , and stir in the soy sauce , ginger and chilli sauce ; set aside.
2. Heat half of the oil in a wok or large frying pan over high heat.
3. Stir in the chicken ; cook and stir until the chicken is no longer pink , about 5 minutes.
4. Remove the chicken from the wok , and set aside.
5. Pour the remaining tablespoon of oil into the wok , and stir in the onion , green pepper , cashews and water chestnuts.
6. Cook and stir until the chestnuts are hot , and the onion has softened , about 5 minutes more.
7. Stir up the sauce to redistribute the cornflour , then pour into the wok , and bring to the boil.
8. Add the reserved chicken , and stir until the sauce thickens and the chicken is hot.
