======== Original Recipe ========
1. Cut the meat , across the grain , into 5cm wide pieces , and then cut each piece into wafer-thin strips.
2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
7. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
8. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
9. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
10. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
11. Remove with a draining spoon.
12. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
13. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.

2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
7. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
8. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
9. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
10. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
11. Remove with a draining spoon.
12. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
13. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.

2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
7. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
8. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
9. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
10. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
11. Remove with a draining spoon.
12. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
13. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.

2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
7. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
8. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
9. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
10. Remove with a draining spoon.
11. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
12. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
13. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.

2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
5. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
6. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
7. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
8. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
9. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
10. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
11. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
12. Remove with a draining spoon.
13. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
14. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
15. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.

2. Place in a small bowl with the cornflour and five-spice powder.
3. Mix together well to coat the meat.
4. Cut the carrot and leek into 5cm lengths , then cut each piece into fine matchsticks.
5. Put all the prepared vegetables together in a bowl.
6. Soak the noodles in a bowl of lightly salted , boiling water for 3 minutes.
7. Serve immediately , with extra soy sauce to sprinkle.
8. Drain and refresh in cold water , then drain again.
9. Combine the soy sauce , sherry and 2 tbsp water in a small jug or bowl.
10. Place a wok or a large frying pan over a high heat.
11. When hot , add 1 tbsp of the oil , then when it is just smoking , toss in the meat and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until cooked through.
12. Remove with a draining spoon.
13. Add the remaining oil with the garlic , ginger and chilli.
14. Stir-fry for 30 seconds , then add the vegetables and toss these around for another 30 seconds.
15. Stir in the soy sauce mixture and as soon as the liquid is bubbling , mix in the drained noodles and finally the meat.
