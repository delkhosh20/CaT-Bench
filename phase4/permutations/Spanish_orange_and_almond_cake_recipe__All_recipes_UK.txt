========
1. Put the chopped oranges in a small saucepan , discarding any pips.
2. Add 1 tbsp water , then cover and cook gently for 30 minutes or until the oranges are soft and excess liquid has evaporated.
3. Leave to cool.
4. Preheat the oven to 180ºC ( 350ºF , gas mark 4 ).
5. Line the bottom and sides of a 23 cm ( 9 in ) springform cake tin with baking parchment.
6. Finely chop the oranges in a food processor or blender , or with a large knife.
7. Put the egg whites in a large bowl and whisk until they form stiff peaks.
8. Gradually whisk in half the caster sugar , then whisk for 1 minute.
9. Using the same whisk , whisk the egg yolks with the remaining caster sugar in another bowl for 2-3 minutes or until pale and quite thick.
10. Whisk in the finely chopped oranges , then carefully fold in the ground almonds.
11. Stir in 3 spoonfuls of the whisked egg white to loosen the mixture , then gently fold in the remaining whites with a large metal spoon.
12. Transfer the mixture to the prepared tin and level the top.
13. Sprinkle with the flaked almonds.
14. Bake for 50-55 minutes or until the cake is golden and a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.
15. Check the cake after 20 minutes and again at 30 minutes , and cover lightly with foil if it is browning too quickly.
16. Leave the cake to cool in the tin , then turn it out , peel away the lining paper and transfer to a serving plate.
17. Dust with icing sugar before serving.
18. The cake can be kept in an airtight tin for up to 2 days.
