========
1. Preheat oven to 200 C / Gas 6.
2. Mix together olive oil , salt , pepper , garlic , chillies and vinegar in a large baking dish.
3. Swirl the tomatoes in the mixture to coat , then arrange cut side up.
4. Sprinkle the tomatoes with a little sugar and bake in preheated oven for 1 hour.
5. Puree everything together and pass through a sieve.
6. Pour into sterilised jars until needed.
