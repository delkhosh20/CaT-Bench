======== Original Recipe ========
1. In a stockpot , combine the blackberries , sugar , lemon juice and salt.
2. Bring to a rolling boil , and cook stirring frequently for 15 to 20 minutes , or until the temperature of the mixture has reached 105 degrees C.
3. Transfer the mixture to hot sterile jars , leaving 1cm headspace , and seal.
4. Process jars for 10 minutes in a water bath.
5. Refrigerate jam once the seal is broken.
