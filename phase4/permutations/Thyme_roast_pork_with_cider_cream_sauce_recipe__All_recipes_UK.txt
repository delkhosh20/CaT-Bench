========
1. Preheat the oven to 200 C / gas 6.
2. Once pork joint is cleaned , rub salt into the skin of the pork to help create a crisp crackling.
3. Put the thyme on the base of a roasting tin with the pork on top ; roast for 45 minutes.
4. Parboil the parsnips for 5 minutes , adding them to the roasting tin when the 45 minutes is up , making sure you cover them in some of the pork 's cooking juices.
5. Roast for a further 45 minutes.
6. Remove pork and parsnips from oven and leave to rest for 10 minutes.
7. Remove thyme from roasting tray along with any excess fat.
8. Add cider to the meat juices , and boil for 2-3 minutes.
9. Stir in the cream , heat through but do not boil.
10. Slice the pork loin and serve with the parsnips and cider cream.
11. Enjoy !
