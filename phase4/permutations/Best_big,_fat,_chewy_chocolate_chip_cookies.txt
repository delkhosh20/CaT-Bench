========
1. 1. Preheat the oven to 170 C / Gas mark 3.
2. 2. Grease baking trays or line with parchment.
3. 3. Sift together the flour , bicarb and salt ; set aside.
4. 4. In a medium bowl , cream together the melted butter , brown sugar and caster sugar until well blended.
5. 5. Beat in the vanilla , egg and egg yolk until light and creamy.
6. 6. Mix in the sifted ingredients until just blended.
7. 7. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand using a wooden spoon.
8. 8. Drop cookie dough onto the prepared baking trays , with each cookie around 4 tablespoons of dough ( for smaller cookies , drop 1 rounded tablespoonful and adjust baking time as necessary ).
11. 9. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes in the preheated oven , or until the edges are lightly toasted.
10. 10. Cookies should be about 8cm apart.
11. 11. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes in the preheated oven , or until the edges are lightly toasted.
12. 12. Cool on baking trays for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
