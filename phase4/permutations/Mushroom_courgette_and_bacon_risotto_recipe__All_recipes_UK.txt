========
1. In a saucepan , keep the chicken stock warm over low heat.
2. In another saucepan , heat a drizzle of olive oil and saute the mushrooms until they 've browned and lost their water.
3. Set aside.
4. Add another drizzle of olive oil and fry courgettes until soft and golden.
5. Set aside.
6. Add 1/2 of the butter and fry shallots until soft.
7. Add remaining butter , add rice stir to coat.
8. Once rice has taken on a pale golden colour , add white wine , stirring continuously until absorbed.
9. Add approximately 200ml of chicken stock to rice , stirring continuously until absorbed.
10. Repeat adding approximately 200ml of chicken stock at a time until the liquid is absorbed.
11. Rice should be soft but al dente.
12. While repeating the stock process , fry diced bacon in a separate pan and mix into the rice at the end.
13. Add cheese , salt and pepper to taste , and stir in.
14. Reheat courgettes and mushrooms , either microwave or in saucepan , and add at the end.
15. Serve with a side salad.
