======== Original Recipe ========
1. Cook the potatoes in a saucepan of lightly salted , boiling water for 10 minutes.
2. Drain and set aside.
3. Meanwhile , heat the oil in a large heavy-based saucepan , add the onion and fry gently for 5 minutes , stirring occasionally.
4. Add the garlic and curry paste and cook for a further 2 minutes , stirring all the time.
5. Add the creamed coconut and stir until it has melted , then add the tomatoes with their juice.
6. Simmer uncovered for 2-3 minutes until the mixture resembles a fairly thick paste.
7. Add the carrots , cauliflower florets and stock , bring to the boil , then cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
8. Add the beans and potatoes and cook for a further 8 minutes.
9. Stir in the prawns and simmer for 2-3 minutes or until they turn pink ( simply heat through for 1-2 minutes if using cooked ).
10. Season to taste , stir in the chopped coriander and serve straight away.
