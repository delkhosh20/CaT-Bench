========
1. Place broccoli in a steamer over boiling water , and cover.
2. Cook until tender but still firm , about 5 minutes.
3. Heat the oil in a frying pan or wok over medium heat , and stir-fry the chicken , spring onions and garlic until the chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
4. Stir the hoisin sauce , chilli paste and soy sauce into the frying pan.
5. Season with ginger , chillies , salt and black pepper.
6. Stir in the chicken stock and simmer about 2 minutes.
7. Mix in the steamed broccoli until coated with the sauce mixture.
