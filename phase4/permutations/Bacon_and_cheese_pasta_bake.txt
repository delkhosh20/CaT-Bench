========
1. 1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4.
2. 2. Place bacon in a large , deep frying pan.
3. 3. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
4. 4. Drain , crumble and set aside.
5. 5. In a large pot with boiling salted water , cook pasta until al dente.
6. 6. Drain.
7. 7. In a medium frying pan , saute the chopped onion and garlic.
8. 8. Take off heat and add chopped cooked bacon and set aside.
9. 9. To make the sauce , in a medium saucepan melt the butter over low heat.
10. 10. Once melted , add the flour and stir constantly for 2 minutes.
11. 11. Gradually add milk and continue stirring until thickened.
12. 12. Stir in 2/3 of the grated Cheddar cheese and stir until melted.
13. 13. Combine cooked pasta , sauteed vegetables and sauce.
14. 14. Pour into a 2 litre baking dish.
15. 15. Sprinkle over remaining Cheddar cheese.
16. 16. Bake uncovered in preheated oven until cheese on top is melted and brown , 15 to 20 minutes.
17. 17. Serve warm.
