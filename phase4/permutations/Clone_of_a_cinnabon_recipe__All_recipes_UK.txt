========
1. Place ingredients for dough in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
2. Select dough cycle ; press Start.
3. After the dough has doubled in size turn it out onto a lightly floured surface , cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
4. In a small bowl , combine brown sugar / cinnamon.
5. Roll dough into a 40x50cm ( 18x20 in ) rectangle.
6. Spread dough with 75g butter and sprinkle evenly with sugar / cinnamon mixture.
7. Roll up dough and cut into 12 slices.
8. Place buns in a lightly greased 23x33cm ( 9x13 in ) baking tin.
9. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled , about 30 minutes.
10. Meanwhile , preheat oven to 200 C / Gas mark 6.
11. Bake buns in preheated oven until golden brown , about 15 minutes.
12. While buns are baking , beat together cream cheese , 50g butter , icing sugar , vanilla and salt.
13. Spread icing on warm rolls before serving.
