1. 2. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4.
3. 3. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes , or until al dente ; drain.
4. 4. Bring 125ml water to the boil in a saucepan , and cook the spinach 4 to 6 minutes , until tender.
5. 5. Place the cooked pasta in a medium bowl.
6. 6. In a small bowl , whisk together the eggs and oil.
7. 7. Toss the pasta with the cooked spinach , egg mixture and breadcrumbs.

9. 9. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the baking dish , and cover with another 1/3 of the pasta sauce.

11. 11. Layer with remaining pasta mixture , and top with remaining sauce.
12. 12. Sprinkle with the rest of the Cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.
13. 13. Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven , or until bubbly and lightly browned.

3. 3. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes , or until al dente ; drain.
4. 4. Bring 125ml water to the boil in a saucepan , and cook the spinach 4 to 6 minutes , until tender.
8. 5. Cover the bottom of a 22x33cm baking dish with 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
5. 6. Place the cooked pasta in a medium bowl.
6. 7. In a small bowl , whisk together the eggs and oil.
7. 8. Toss the pasta with the cooked spinach , egg mixture and breadcrumbs.
9. 9. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the baking dish , and cover with another 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
10. 10. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the Cheddar and 1/2 of the mozzarella.
11. 11. Layer with remaining pasta mixture , and top with remaining sauce.
12. 12. Sprinkle with the rest of the Cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.
13. 13. Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven , or until bubbly and lightly browned.
