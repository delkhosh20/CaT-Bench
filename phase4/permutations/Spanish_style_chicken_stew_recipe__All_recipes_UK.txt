========
1. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Stir in onions and garlic , cook until the onion has softened and turned translucent.
3. Pour in tomatoes , chickpeas and water ; season with paprika , chilli flakes , salt , and pepper.
4. Stir in carrots and potatoes , then place chicken thighs skin-side up on top of the vegetables.
5. Bring to a boil over high heat , then reduce heat to medium , cover and simmer until the chicken is tender , about 25 minutes.
6. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C / gas mark 6.
7. Remove chicken from the stew and set aside.
8. Stir in the chorizo slices , then pour the stew into a baking dish.
9. Place the chicken thighs on top , skin-side up.
10. Bake in preheated oven until the stew has thickened and the chicken skin is crispy , about 15 minutes.
