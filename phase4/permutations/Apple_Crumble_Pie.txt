========
1. Preheat oven to 200 C / Gas 6.
2. Arrange apple slices in unbaked pastry case.
3. Mix 100g sugar and cinnamon ; sprinkle over apples.
4. Mix 5 tbsp sugar with flour ; rub in butter until crumbly.
5. Spoon mixture over apples.
6. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes , or until apples are soft and top is lightly browned.
