========
1. In a large , glass bowl , stir together the olive oil , lemon juice , parsley , garlic and black pepper.
2. Season with crushed chillies , if desired.
3. Add prawns , and toss to coat.
4. Marinate in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
5. Preheat barbecue for high heat.
6. Thread prawns onto skewers , piercing once near the tail and once near the head.
7. Discard any remaining marinade.
8. Lightly oil cooking grate.
6. Thread prawns onto skewers , piercing once near the tail and once near the head.
9. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes per side , or until opaque.
