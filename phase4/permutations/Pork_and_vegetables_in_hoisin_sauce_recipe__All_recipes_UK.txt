========
1. Make the marinade by mixing together the sugar , salt , soy sauce and cornflour slurry.
2. Mix in the pork , let marinate for 15 minutes.
3. Meanwhile , blanch the cabbage and carrots in boiling water for 3 minutes.
4. Rinse under cold water to cool.
5. In a small bowl , mix together the hoisin sauce , oyster sauce , sugar and shaoxing wine to make the sauce.
6. Set aside.
7. In a large frying pan , heat 1 tablespoon oil over high heat.
8. Stir-fry the pork until done , 4-5 minutes.
9. Set aside.
10. Heat the same frying pan with 1 tablespoon oil over high heat.
11. Stir-fry the garlic and shallots until fragrant , 3-4 minutes.
12. Stir in the onion and salt , cook until soft , 3-4 minutes.
13. Stir in the cabbage , carrots , water chestnuts , green pepper , pork and sauce.
14. Heat through for about 2-3 minutes.
15. Serve.
