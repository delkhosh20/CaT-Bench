1. 1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. 2. Saute the carrots and onion for a few minutes until the onion has softened a little.
3. 3. Pour in the vegetable stock and add the coriander.
4. 4. Bring to the boil , and cook until the carrots are tender , about 10 minutes.
5. 5. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly.
6. 6. Puree the soup until smooth , using a hand blender or food processor.
7. 7. Reheat before serving if necessary.
8. 8. Serve with crusty bread.
