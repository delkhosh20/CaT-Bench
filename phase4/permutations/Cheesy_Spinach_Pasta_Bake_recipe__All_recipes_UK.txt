========
1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to the boil.
3. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes , or until al dente ; drain.
4. Bring 125ml water to the boil in a saucepan , and cook the spinach 4 to 6 minutes , until tender.
5. Place the cooked pasta in a medium bowl.
6. In a small bowl , whisk together the eggs and oil.
7. Toss the pasta with the cooked spinach , egg mixture and breadcrumbs.
8. Cover the bottom of a 22x33cm baking dish with 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
9. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the baking dish , and cover with another 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
10. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the Cheddar and 1/2 of the mozzarella.
11. Layer with remaining pasta mixture , and top with remaining sauce.
12. Sprinkle with the rest of the Cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.
13. Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven , or until bubbly and lightly browned.

3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to the boil.
4. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes , or until al dente ; drain.
5. Bring 125ml water to the boil in a saucepan , and cook the spinach 4 to 6 minutes , until tender.
6. In a small bowl , whisk together the eggs and oil.
7. Toss the pasta with the cooked spinach , egg mixture and breadcrumbs.
8. Cover the bottom of a 22x33cm baking dish with 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
9. Pour half of the pasta mixture into the baking dish , and cover with another 1/3 of the pasta sauce.
10. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the Cheddar and 1/2 of the mozzarella.
11. Layer with remaining pasta mixture , and top with remaining sauce.
12. Sprinkle with the rest of the Cheddar and mozzarella cheeses.
13. Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven , or until bubbly and lightly browned.
