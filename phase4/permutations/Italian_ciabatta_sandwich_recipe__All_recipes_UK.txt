========
1. Slice bread in half lengthways.
2. Drizzle olive oil on both cut sides.
3. Sprinkle both sides with Parmesan cheese , basil and oregano.
4. On the bottom half , layer black and green olives , then the salami , ham , provolone and mozzarella.
5. Cover with top layer , and cut into 4 servings.
