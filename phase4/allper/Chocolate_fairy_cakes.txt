========
1. 1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C ( gas mark 4 ).
2. 2. Line a bun tray with paper or foil cases.
3. 3. Sift together the flour , baking powder , bicarbonate of soda , cocoa and salt.
4. 4. Set aside.
5. 5. In a large bowl , cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
6. 6. Add the eggs one at a time , beating well with each addition , then stir in the vanilla.
7. 7. Add the flour mixture alternately with the milk ; beat well.
8. 8. Fill the paper cups 3/4's full.
9. 9. Bake for 15 minutes in the preheated oven , or until a skewer inserted into the cake comes out clean.
10. 10. Ice with your favourite icing when cool.
